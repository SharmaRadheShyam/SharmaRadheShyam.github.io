Radhe Shyam Sharma

 

Dr. Radhe Shyam Sharma
Assistant Professor
School of Computing and Electrical Engineering
IIT Mandi

Before joining IIT Mandi, I was working as an Autonomy Senior Engineer in the Integrated Components and Solutions Division at Caterpillar. I obtained PhD in 2020 from the Department of Electrical Engineering, at IIT Kanpur. I have pursued my doctoral research under the supervision of Prof. Laxmidhar Behera and Prof. Venkatesh K S. My research interests are broadly in the field of Robotics, Vision, Control and Automation with emphasis on the visual servoing, robot learning from demontration, multi-robot systems, modeling and simulation.
